About Us

We are the electricity company of the future

Magic⚡Power is connecting homes and businesses to reliable and affordable solar powered electricity that will never run out. No more power cuts, Enjoy 24/7 electricity for your home and workplace.

Impact

Making an Impact

We are proud to be making a huge difference in the lives of our users

Affordable and clean energy (SDG7)

Our goal is to become a multi-national provider of clean, affordable energy products, designed to reach even the most remote customers.

Decent work and economic growth (SDG8)

Magic⚡Power is bridging the gap of electricity access that has hindered employment and economic growth in many African communities

No poverty (SDG1)

By having access to power, we are making it easier for families to escape poverty, as they can build a business from home, educate children and better managing their health.

Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure (SDG9)

Magic⚡Power offers efficient and affordable power to awaken the next generation of entrepreneurs, and inspire creativity across across Africa.

Climate action (SDG13)

Magic⚡Power is taking the fight against carbon emissions very seriously. Our solar power systems help our customers to reduce carbon emissions and protect the planet.

Sustainable cities and communities (SDG11)

Our mission is to builds resilient and vibrant communities of tomorrow that unleash the potential of Africa's people and its natural resources.
